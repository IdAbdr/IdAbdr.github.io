Welcome to "Owl Cafe" - Your Cozy Haven Amongst Owls!

A st., 32

L st., 60/1

"Owl Cafe" is a unique place where you can experience the joy of interacting with friendly and enigmatic owls. We've crafted this space to bring together individuals who share our passion for these fascinating birds.
Our feathered companions are the true stars of the cafe! We feature a variety of owl breeds, each with its own unique personality. You can get to know each one, learn their stories, and even have the chance to pet them if they're willing.
