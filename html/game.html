Rules of the Game

The goal of the game: Collect the letters "F," "L,", "A", etc. and drag them to the corresponding slots.

The beginning of the game: At the beginning of the game, the letters "F," "L,", "A", etc. are on the left, and the letter slots are on the right.

Dragging: You can drag the letters by clicking and holding on the letter with the mouse. Drag a letter into one of the slots.

Check: If a letter is dragged into the correct slot (for example, the letter "F" into the slot for "F"), it locks in place and you move on to the next letter.

Game Completion: To complete the game successfully, all letters must be dragged into the correct slots. When all the letters are in the correct slots, a victory message is displayed.

Try again: If the letters are dragged into the wrong slots, you can try again by dragging them into the correct slots.

Victory: If the letters are not pulled out, you win!

Have fun and enjoy the game!
